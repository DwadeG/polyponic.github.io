Behold! This is the Beyond Lettuce story!

Okay, so what is this?

Beyond Lettuce offers you organic, heirloom produce you can afford with the unique experience of live harvest. It looks fantastic. It tastes fantastic. And if it looks like a duck...

Nice! What is Live Harvest?

It's only the freshest, healthiest produce money can buy. Live produce keeps growing right up until it’s harvested- by you, in person, right when you buy it. This produce is fresh. Not even today-fresh. Right now fresh. No kidding.

ZipGrow©? What's that?

Zipgrow© is vertical growing technology for the modern farmer. Bright Agrotech. These guys make it so we can keep all our produce right where it grows. Right where you will buy it. So you will eat well tonight.

Your food. Where did it come from? Who grew it? What methods did they use? Where has it been? For how long? Remove the guess work.

I don’t want to say it’s the aliens ...
but it’s the aliens.

I mean isn't it possible?

We know how frustrating it can be. Figuring out where your food came from is no easy task. With the growing concerns about how food is grown and the effect it has on the surrounding area, consumers are demanding some accountability. At Beyond Lettuce, we plant the seeds, grow and harvest your food right where you see them. No congressional oversight needed. Want to know more? Where did the seeds come from? Do they include GMO's? (The answer is no.) How do I harvest? Just ask! Any of our associates will provide you with details about each plant, including how best to incorporate it into your next meal.
