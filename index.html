Behold! This is the Beyond Lettuce story!

Okay, so what is this?

Beyond Lettuce offers urban foodies organic, heirloom produce at competitive prices and with the unique experience of live harvest.

Nice! What is Live Harvest?

It's the freshest, healthiest produce money can buy. Live produce keeps growing right up until it’s harvested- by you. This produce didn’t sit on a truck or a shelf for days or weeks. This produce is fresh. Not even today-fresh. Right now fresh.

ZipGrow©? What's that?

Zipgrow© is vertical growing technology for the modern farmer. The tower growing method allows us to bring our produce to market where customers like you do the harvesting. It doesn't get more fresh than that. Bright Agrotech
