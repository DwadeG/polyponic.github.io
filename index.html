The Beyond Lettuce story.

What we do.

We support farmers who keep our food and our money in our community. We specialize in hydroponic, aquaponic, and vermiponic systems. We can design stand-alone systems or integrate into existing farms.

What is Live Harvest?

One of the unique benefits of many hydroponic systems is the ability to sell produce at it's best. Vertical tower systems allow you to transport your produce to market in the container they are grown in. Your customers can harvest directly from the tower and get the best produce available.

Hydroponics with ZipGrow©

Zipgrow© is a vertical hydroponic system from Bright Agrotech. We have yet to find a better system for growing live harvest produce.

Your food. Where did it come from? Who grew it? What methods did they use? Where has it been? For how long? Remove the guess work.

No one asks for harmful substances in their food. Transparency in food production should be obvious but it certainly is not.

Isn't it possible?

We know how frustrating it can be. Trying to find out where your food came from is no easy task. With the growing concerns about how food is grown and the effects of chemical fertilizer use, consumers are demanding some accountability.
