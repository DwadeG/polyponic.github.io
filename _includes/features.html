What is Polyponic?

Innovative

Polyponics is an Aquaponic/Hydroponic system teamed up with compost worms to create a modern advancement in how fruits and vegetables are grown

Self Sustained

This system eliminates the need to water, fertilize and deweed your plants.

Costomized

Build a Polyponic System right in your home, completely customized to cater to the space and decor of your home.
